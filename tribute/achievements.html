Major Achievements of Dr. Abdul Qadeer Khan

  • Founder of Pakistan’s Nuclear Program – known as the "Father of Pakistan’s Atomic Bomb".
  • Established Kahuta Research Laboratories (KRL), a leading nuclear research facility.
  • Played a key role in making Pakistan the first Islamic country with nuclear weapons (1998).
  • Awarded Nishan-e-Imtiaz twice for his outstanding contributions.
  • Recipient of Hilal-e-Imtiaz, another prestigious national award.
  • Strengthened Pakistan’s defense and gave it a strong position on the world stage.
  • Contributed to scientific research in metallurgy and uranium enrichment technology.
  • Inspired generations of young Pakistani scientists and engineers.
