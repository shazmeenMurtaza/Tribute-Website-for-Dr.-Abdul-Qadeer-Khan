Timeline of Dr. Abdul Qadeer Khan

  • 1936 – Born in Bhopal, India.
  • 1952 – Migrated to Pakistan after Partition.
  • 1960s – Completed higher education in Europe, specializing in metallurgy.
  • 1974 – Returned to Pakistan after India’s nuclear test, determined to develop nuclear capability.
  • 1976 – Founded Kahuta Research Laboratories (KRL).
  • 1998 – Played a central role in Pakistan’s successful nuclear tests (Chagai-I & II).
  • 1999 – Awarded Nishan-e-Imtiaz for the second time.
  • 2004 – Retired from KRL but remained a national hero.
  • 2021 – Passed away on October 10, leaving a legacy of scientific achievement.
